Overview

From payment confusion to clear records

AnjohLedger helps treasurers and financial secretaries process payment declarations from WhatsApp, text messages, emails, and payment alerts into one simple review screen.

What this demo will show

  • The current chaos of scattered messages and repeated confirmations.
  • How records can be processed step by step.
  • How likely duplicates and unclear items can be surfaced for review.
  • A clean summary of who has paid, what needs review, and what is still pending.

Important note

This is a demonstration prototype. It simulates the workflow using realistic sample records. It does not yet connect to live WhatsApp, email, SMS, or payment apps.

  • 14 sample records
  • User-controlled screen progression
  • Two short research prompts
  • WhatsApp CTA at the end
Built as a static web app for easy deployment on Vercel.
